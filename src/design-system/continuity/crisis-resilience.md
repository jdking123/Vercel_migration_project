# Crisis Resilience

The design system must remain stable under external pressure or crisis.

## 1. Crisis-Resilient Communication Patterns
- emergency communication templates
- uncertainty disclosure structures
- risk-based visual hierarchy
- public-facing clarity components

## 2. Independence Under Scrutiny
The system must:
- document evidence sources
- publish decision rationales
- maintain transparency logs

## 3. Stability Requirements
The design system must remain consistent during:
- regulatory change
- political pressure
- industry pressure
- societal crises
- technological disruption

Resilience is a governance requirement.
