# Intergenerational Responsibility

The design system must protect future practitioners, regulators, organizations, and the public.

## 1. Ethical Defaults
- evidence-based communication
- risk-aware hierarchy
- uncertainty disclosure
- avoidance of manipulation

## 2. Accessibility as a Moral Requirement
Accessibility is not compliance; it is responsibility.

## 3. Global Applicability
Patterns must support:
- multilingual parity
- cross-cultural clarity
- jurisdiction-neutral communication

## 4. Cross-Generational Safeguards
All decisions must consider:
- long-term impact
- future regulatory evolution
- societal consequences
- protection of those affected by product decisions

The design system is a legacy instrument.
