# Stewardship Roles

The design system is maintained through three permanent stewardship roles that persist across generations.

## 1. Design Steward
Responsible for:
- visual integrity
- accessibility compliance
- editorial clarity
- identity alignment

## 2. Engineering Steward
Responsible for:
- architectural integrity
- performance and reliability
- token and component enforcement
- interoperability and extensibility

## 3. Governance Steward
Responsible for:
- standards alignment
- ethical and evidence-based communication
- drift prevention
- audit and review cycles

These roles ensure continuity regardless of individual contributors.
