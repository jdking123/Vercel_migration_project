# Stewardship Cycles

Long-term review cycles ensure the design system remains aligned with the Foundation’s identity and mission.

## 1. Annual Stewardship Review
Assesses:
- identity alignment
- governance performance
- drift indicators
- global applicability
- accessibility performance

## 2. Triennial Strategic Review
Assesses:
- alignment with Mission and Vision
- global professional needs
- regulatory evolution
- capability building requirements

## 3. Five-Year Institutional Review
Assesses:
- alignment with Purpose
- global trust and recognition
- profession-wide maturity
- long-term sustainability

These cycles ensure evolution without drift.
