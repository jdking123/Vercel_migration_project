# Leadership Neutrality

The design system must remain stable regardless of leadership changes.

## 1. Leadership-Agnostic Governance
Governance structures must not depend on:
- leadership preferences
- board composition
- committee turnover
- external pressure

## 2. Policy-Linked Decision Structures
Design system governance is tied to:
- organizational policy
- standards policy
- regulatory intelligence policy
- capability building policy

Leadership cannot override governance without formal review.

## 3. Independence Safeguards
- conflict-of-interest protocols
- external audit cycles
- transparency requirements
- public accountability mechanisms

Neutrality is structurally protected.
