# Design System Stewardship Charter

## Purpose
Define long-term ownership and accountability.

## Principles
- Stability
- Accessibility
- Transparency
- Predictability
- Multilingual parity
- Institutional trust

## Roles
- Design stewards
- Engineering stewards
- Accessibility stewards
- Governance committee

## Cadence
- Monthly stewardship meeting
- Quarterly governance review
- Annual system audit
