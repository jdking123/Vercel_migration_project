# Stakeholder Accountability

The Foundation works alongside industry, regulators, and civil society — not for any one of them.

## 1. Industry Accountability
- no privileged access
- no commercial influence
- no industry-specific bias

## 2. Regulator Accountability
- no regulatory capture
- no implied endorsement
- no jurisdictional favoritism

## 3. Civil Society Accountability
- transparent communication
- ethical clarity
- public accessibility

Accountability ensures balanced, global legitimacy.
