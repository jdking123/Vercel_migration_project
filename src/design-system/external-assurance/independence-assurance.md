# Independence Assurance

This document defines the mechanisms that ensure the Foundation’s design system remains free from commercial, political, or ideological influence.

## 1. Independence Requirements
The design system must:
- avoid persuasive or advocacy-oriented design
- maintain neutral tone and presentation
- prevent stakeholder-specific bias
- enforce transparent sourcing and citations

## 2. Independence Controls
- public governance logs
- conflict-of-interest protocols
- external review of major decisions
- transparent methodology for regulatory intelligence

## 3. Independence Verification
Independence is verified through:
- annual external audit
- public reporting
- evidence traceability
- identity spine alignment

Independence must be verifiable, not merely claimed.
