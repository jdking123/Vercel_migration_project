# Ethical Assurance

This document defines the mechanisms that ensure the design system protects those affected by product decisions.

## 1. Ethical Communication
The system must:
- disclose uncertainty
- avoid manipulation
- present risk clearly
- prioritize public understanding

## 2. Accessibility as Ethics
Accessibility is:
- a moral requirement
- a global obligation
- a cross-generational responsibility

## 3. Ethical Safeguards
- risk-based visual hierarchy
- ethical review of major updates
- impact statements for standards and guidance

Ethical assurance protects present and future generations.
