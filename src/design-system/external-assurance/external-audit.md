# External Audit & Verification

This document defines the audit mechanisms that verify independence, evidence integrity, and global trustworthiness.

## 1. Annual External Audit
Assesses:
- independence
- evidence integrity
- accessibility compliance
- global applicability
- ethical alignment

## 2. Standards Verification
Ensures:
- harmonization
- cross-jurisdictional clarity
- traceability
- version integrity

## 3. Regulatory Intelligence Verification
Ensures:
- neutrality
- evidence hierarchy
- uncertainty disclosure
- absence of bias

Auditability is essential to global trust.
