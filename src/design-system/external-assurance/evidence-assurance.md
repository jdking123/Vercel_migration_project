# Evidence-Based Assurance

This document defines how the design system ensures that all outputs are grounded in evidence, not opinion.

## 1. Evidence Hierarchy
The design system must support:
- structured evidence tiers
- clear distinction between fact and interpretation
- uncertainty disclosure
- citation integrity

## 2. Editorial Requirements
- sober, neutral tone
- no dramatization or embellishment
- consistent regulatory comparison structures
- traceable methodology

## 3. Verification
Evidence alignment is verified through:
- external audit
- public methodology disclosure
- versioned regulatory intelligence templates

Evidence is a structural requirement.
