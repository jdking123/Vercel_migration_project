# Mission Alignment

The design system operationalizes the Foundation’s Mission by enabling:

## Standards & Ethics
- Harmonized, globally tiered standards
- Ethical, neutral, and traceable communication
- Templates for standards, annexes, and guidance

## Regulatory Intelligence
- Evidence hierarchy and uncertainty disclosure
- Jurisdictional comparison structures
- Sober, authoritative editorial tone

## Capability Building
- Learning pathway templates
- Certification and assessment patterns
- Multilingual accessibility and global reach

The design system is the operational backbone of the Mission.
